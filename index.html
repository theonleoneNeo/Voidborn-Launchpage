Boot up a retro-fueled universe simulation! Watch NPCs colonize a 4000x3000 grid, forge factions, climb tech tiers, and battle cosmic chaos. In God Mode, wield Observer Points to unleash calamities, summon UFOs, or spark fertility zones!

Game Features
  • Procedural Universe: Navigate a massive 4000x3000 grid teeming with resources, buildings, and dynamic NPCs.
  • Tech Progression: Evolve from Pioneer to Galactic with farms, mines, temples, factories, portals, and epic wonders.
  • God Mode: Spend Observer Points to trigger blackholes, UFO boons, or fertility zones to shape the simulation.
  • Factions & Events: Witness faction wars, alliances, and cosmic events like UFO visits or cataclysmic disasters.
  • Retro 90s UI: Rock a Windows 95-style interface with minimaps, event logs, and colony stats.
  • Exploration & Building: NPCs with Explorer, Builder, or Warrior traits roam, construct, and unearth rare artifacts.
